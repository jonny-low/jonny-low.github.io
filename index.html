I'm a lecturer / researcher in the Department of Software System and Cybersecurity (SSC), Faculty of IT, Monash University Australia. From computer engineering specialised in computer networks background and having passion to ease our daily life through technology, I’ve developed interest in digital health and currently affiliated to HumaniSE Lab working on my PhD research to help our healthcare front liner coping with mental health patients through software engineering. I also have experiences in different technical and management roles managing customer's enterprise IT infrastructures for Fortune 500 companies and analysing customer's business use cases to deliver ideas through Proof-of-Concept projects for end to end solutions with the aim to improve service delivery operations.I was a Senior Researcher in Telekom Research & Development (subsidiary of Telekom Malaysia) and Global Network Delivery Manager in Hewlett-Packard Enterprise Services (now known as DXC Technology) prior to that. A volunteer with IEEE and as an adult leader with Scouts Victoria.
